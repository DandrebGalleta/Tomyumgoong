Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classing Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp.

How to make Tom Yum Goong

Ingredients:

  • Lemongrass: 2 stalks
  • Thai basil: 1 cup
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves: 3 leaves
  • Shrimp: 500g
    •

Preparation:

  • Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  • Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  • Stir in shrimp and cook until pink.
  • Season with fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.
  • Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot.

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4 cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor.
  • 1 tablesoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat.
